How to Play Charades With Charade Generator

1. Select how many charades you want on screen and start the game you play.
First, decide how many charades (words or phrases) you want to appear on the screen at one time. This helps you control the game length and difficulty. After selecting the number, start the game to begin playing.

2. You should also select custom charades for display on screen.
You can choose custom charades based on your preference, such as movies, actions, animals, professions, or funny phrases. This makes the game more interesting and suitable for different age groups or themes.

3. Full screen option is also here, you can play after clicking on the full screen button.
The full screen option allows the game to fill the entire screen. This is useful when playing with a group, as everyone can clearly see the charade without distractions from other buttons or content.

4. Click Generate
Once your selections are complete, click the Generate button. The system will randomly generate the charades and display them on the screen, ready to be acted out.

5. Act the charade without speaking
The player must act out the displayed charade using only body movements, facial expressions, and actions. Speaking, making sounds, or mouthing words is not allowed.

6. Use gestures and expressions
Use clear hand movements, body language, and facial expressions to give clues. Creative gestures make it easier for others to understand and guess correctly.

7. Let others guess
The remaining players watch the performance and try to guess the charade. The first person to guess correctly wins that round, or the team scores a point, depending on the rules you choose.
